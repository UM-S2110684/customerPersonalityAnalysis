Introduction

Customer Personality Analysis is a shiny app that aim to to analyse data from users based on age, education, marital status, yearly Household income, category of product, number of purchases made with a discount.

~Purpose~

  • Help Entrepreneurs to target their customers based on the buying behaviour that analysed from the datasets.

  • Help Business campany to make suitable discounts to achieve their business goals based on the output graph.

Analysis Algorithm

1.read csv file, EDA

2.Remove the non-informative data from datasets through data cleaning process

3.Declare Marital Status 'Alone', 'YOLO' and 'Absurd' as 'Single'.

4.Calculate the age and total spending on all items

5.Creating a feature on ShinyApp that indicates the number of days customer engaged to the campany

6.Dropping the outliers

7.Encoding the dataframe"Education" and “Marital Status” into numeric forms
